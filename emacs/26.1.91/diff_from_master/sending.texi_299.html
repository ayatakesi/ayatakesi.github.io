Mail aliases expand as abbrevs---that is to say, as soon as you type a word-separator character after an alias (@pxref{Abbrevs}). This expansion takes place only within the @samp{To}, @samp{From}, @samp{CC}, @samp{BCC}, and @samp{Reply-to} @samp{Reply-To} header fields (plus their @samp{Resent-} variants); it does not take place in other header fields, such as @samp{Subject}.
